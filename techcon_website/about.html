History

TechCon was founded in 2015 with a vision to bring together the brightest minds in technology. What started as a small gathering of 100 attendees has grown into one of the world's premier technology conferences, attracting over 5,000 participants from 50+ countries. Each year, we continue to push boundaries and showcase the latest in technology.

Mission

TechCon 2024 is dedicated to bringing together the brightest minds in technology to share knowledge, inspire innovation, and foster collaboration. Our mission is to create an inclusive environment where technology professionals can learn, grow, and connect. We believe in the power of technology to solve global challenges and create positive change in the world.
